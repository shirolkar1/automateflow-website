Frequently asked questions.

Intelligent Document Processing (IDP) is an advanced form of automation that uses AI, machine learning, and natural language processing to extract, validate, and process data from structured, semi-structured, and unstructured documents. Unlike traditional OCR, IDP understands context and can handle document variations automatically.

IDP can automatically extract key data points from documents, validate the information against business rules, route documents for approval, and integrate the data into downstream systems. It can also flag exceptions, perform compliance checks, and maintain audit trails for regulatory purposes.

IDP can process virtually any document type including invoices, contracts, forms, emails, PDFs, images, handwritten documents, insurance claims, loan applications, medical records, and more. It adapts to document variations and layouts automatically.

Document Automation integrates seamlessly into accounts payable, customer onboarding, claims processing, compliance reviews, contract management, HR processes, and any workflow that involves document-heavy tasks. It works within existing systems through API integrations and workflow orchestration.

IDP powers AI Agents by providing them with structured data extracted from documents. The Process Reasoning Engine (PRE) orchestrates AI Agents to make intelligent decisions based on document content, route work items, trigger approvals, and execute complex multi-step processes across systems.
