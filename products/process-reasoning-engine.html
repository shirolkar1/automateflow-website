Process Reasoning Engine (PRE)

The AI Brain of Enterprise Automation

The Process Reasoning Engine (PRE) is the AI brain behind the Agentic Process Automation (APA) System and agentic solutions—securely orchestrating AI agents, automations, and people to run complex, cross-functional business processes at scale.

Benefits

Automate better.

PRE powers generative automation that delivers 3x higher efficacy for building end-to-end workflows. It enables seamless human-agent collaboration, and sharpens context and reasoning with every run.

More accurate. More resilient.

Increase automation resiliency by 60%. Self-healing, adaptive workflow intelligence enables AI agents to plan, act, and learn nonstop—and extract any data with 95%+ accuracy from any document.

Secure, private, and safe.

Maintain full privacy, compliance, and responsible AI practices. Supports emerging standards like GCP's Agent-to-Agent (A2A) protocol and Anthropic's Model Context Protocol (MCP).

Proven at enterprise scale.

Trained on 400M+ real-world documents and data, to understand how business decisions unfold across complex systems. PRE powers 1,500+ live deployments and 1M+ AI agent executions across finance, healthcare, and more.
