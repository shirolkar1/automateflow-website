Automation Co-Pilot is an AI-powered automation assistant that works directly within your existing business applications. Unlike basic AI chat tools, it doesn't just provide suggestions—it actively generates, builds, and deploys full automations within real enterprise workflows, powered by our Process Reasoning Engine (PRE).

No coding experience is required. Automation Co-Pilot uses natural language processing and conversational AI from Amazon Q Business, allowing you to communicate your automation needs in everyday language. The system interprets your intent and creates the necessary automations automatically.

Automation Co-Pilot can help with various business processes including claims processing, invoice automation, customer service workflows, data entry tasks, report generation, and cross-system integrations. It's particularly effective for repetitive tasks that require coordination across multiple applications like SAP, Workday, and Salesforce.
